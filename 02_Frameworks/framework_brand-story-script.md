# Brand Story Script

This forms the basis of your messaging throughout your website. Going through this with your client to agree on the key messaging will help you to make something that truly connects with their customers.

Reference video: https://youtu.be/Ny-Az8nAAaM?si=KDAj0uYFkkAQ4aVF

---

## A character

Who is your customer? What struggle are they facing right now?

Example: A photographer with a website that isn't effectively attracting potential clients, resulting in a lack of leads and business growth.

## External Problem

This is your customers surface level need. What is your customer typing into Google when they are looking for a service/product like yours?

Example: I need a web designer to help me attract more potential clients.

## Internal Problem

What feelings are they having because they don't have a solution to their external problem?

Example: Feeling frustrated by low budget leads and lack of business growth despite their passion and talent.

## Philosophical Problem

Why is it wrong that your customers would experience this problem? Usually begins with "you shouldn't have to..."

Example: You shouldn't have to work so hard to convince low-budget leads of the value of your work.

## Villain

What's stopping the customer from achieving their goals or desired transformation? Usually related to time, money, mindset or lack of knowledge.

Examples:
- Time: Spending an excessive amount of time and energy chasing new clients
- Money/Mindset: Reluctant to invest in their online presence
- Lack of knowledge: Website is functioning ok, but not great and not sure what the missing piece is

## Positive Outcomes

List the positive outcomes or transformation they'll experience after working with you. This is about results and transformation rather than the features of your product.

Examples:
- Set yourself apart as the go-to expert in your industry
- Raise your brand presence to attract higher budget clients who are excited to work with you
- More time to focus on the parts of your business that you love
- Grow your business and confidently raise your rates

## Plan

Give them a clear step-by-step plan to get from A to B. Keep it simple - if you confuse you lose.

Examples:
1. It starts with a quick chat about your business and the goals for your website.
2. In our strategy session, we'll work with you to craft a custom website strategy based on your long term goals.
3. Leave the rest to us and we'll get to designing. When you're happy we'll help you hit launch on your new website!

## Empathy

How can you use your past experience to show that you understand your customers problems and know how to solve them?

Example: I started my design business to escape the 9-5, but soon enough I was working my ass off 24/7. Struggling to find clients and looking around at everyone else wondering what I was doing wrong. It wasn't until I really got clear on my messaging and learnt how to apply this to my marketing and website, I went from struggling to find clients that would pay me $500 to attracting my first $5000 client within weeks. Anyone can build a website, but I wanna build you a money-making machine that leaves clients EXCITED to throw their money at you.

## Authority

Show that you have what it takes to help your customer overcome those problems.

This is about showing results: past clients, testimonials, case studies, portfolio examples.

## Help them avoid failure

Gently remind your customer what will happen if they fail to solve their problems.

Example: They won't grow or get what they want. Losing money, wasting time, increasing stress, constantly feeling overwhelmed.
