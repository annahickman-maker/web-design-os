# Website Copy Template - eCommerce

Work through each page, filling in each section. Pages are broken into key sections to give a clear guide for how to write each section and the best structure.

---

## Tips
1. Keep it simple - write how you would explain it face to face. Clear over clever.
2. Let go of perfectionism - don't let overthinking stop you. Revise after launch.

## Guidelines
1. People don't read, they scan - short and snappy headlines
2. It's not about you - audience is the hero
3. Call to actions - clear, consistent, always visible

# Home Page

### Benefit Driven Headline
Tell customers what you can do for them. Create a desirable image of how products make their life better.

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action

### Establish Authority
Build trust immediately - display trusted brands, publications, or powerful testimonial.

List quotes, testimonials or brands that have featured your products.

### Featured Collection
Showcase best-sellers or featured products. Include a desirable heading telling people what your product is and why they need it.

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action
List featured products or collections.

### Establish Trust
This section should tell your customer:
1. Why you exist as a brand and what you stand for
2. Your unique value proposition - why pick you over competition
3. You understand and empathise with their problems and know the solution

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action

### Additional Products or Collections
List all products or collections to feature on homepage.

### Testimonials
Curate ones that best express what you want to demonstrate about your products.

Fields: Name, Product, Testimonial

### Blog
Feature top 3 blog posts that address customer problems and demonstrate how products help. Be informational and transparent.

Fields: Blog Title, Excerpt, Blog Content

### Final Call To Action
Desirable outcome and final call to action.

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action
