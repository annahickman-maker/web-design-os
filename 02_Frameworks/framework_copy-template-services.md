# Website Copy Template - Services

Work through each page, filling in each section. The pages are broken into key sections to give a clear guide for how to write each section and the best structure to use.

---

## Tips

1. Keep it simple - write how you would explain it face to face. Clear over clever.
2. Let go of perfectionism - don't let overthinking stop you. You can always revise after launch.

## Guidelines

1. People don't read, they scan - communicate in as few words as possible using short and snappy headlines
2. It's not about you - your audience is the hero. What are they looking for? How can you guide them?
3. Call to actions - tell them what to do next, clear and consistent, always visible

---

# Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

### SEO Foundations

1. List search phrases - all the phrases a potential customer may search that your service can help with
2. Choose your keywords - from the list, choose keywords that best represent your brand. The more specific, the smaller your competition
3. Create valuable content - intercept your customer when looking for solutions. Build trust, establish authority, show why your product is the answer

### Key Phrases
List 5-10 specific phrases your potential customer may be searching for.

### Location
If local business, specify location and focus area.
Is your business registered under Google My Business? Yes/No

### Website SEO Description
Your website description shows up on search engines. Treat it as an advert - make it compelling and relevant.

# Home Page

### Benefit Driven Headline
Tell clients what you can do for them. Create a desirable image of who they aspire to be. Think about what drives them at the deepest level - hint at the emotion or transformation your service offers.

Copy prompts:
- Get [desirable outcome/emotion] without [pain point]
- [Adjective] [service] to help you [get results / feel emotion]

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action

### Building Connection
Address the key problem your service solves. Build connection and show you're the right person.

Copy prompts:
- You're struggling to get [desirable result] because you don't have [missing piece]
- You have [example], but you aren't [achieving this result]
- How does this make them feel?
- Have you been in the same situation? How did you overcome this?

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action

### Work With Me
What are your key offers? Brief explanation of each including main benefits and what type of customer each offer is for.

Fields: Offer Heading, Description, Call to Action

### About Me
Short section introducing who you are. Establish authority and build connection.

Copy prompt: I am a [impressive qualification] that's helped over [X number] of clients achieve [this result]

Fields: Heading, Description

### Case Studies
Use a previous client project to demonstrate results.

Template: When [client] came to me they were struggling with [problem]. So I helped them [brief project outline]. Now they have [positive results].

Fields: Heading, Description, Testimonial

### Testimonials
Template: "Before I was struggling with [problem] and it made me feel [negative emotion], now I have [achieved this result] and I feel [positive emotion]"

Pro tip: Client images/videos and message screenshots feel genuine and authentic.

Fields: Name & Position, Service Type, Testimonial

### Lead Magnet (Freebie)
Offering a freebie builds trust, authority and grows email list. Could be a PDF guide, discount code or free template.

Tip: Check social comments for commonly asked questions your freebie could solve.

Fields: Heading, Description

### Final Call To Action
Finish with a desirable outcome and final call to action.

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action

# Services Page

### Benefit Driven Headline
One hero statement about the transformation your services offer.

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action

### Services Breakdown
Visual breakdown of each offer so customers can easily compare. Link to detailed descriptions or individual sales pages.

Tip: People gravitate towards the middle offer - place your key offer there.

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action

### Show the Transformation
Clear before and after.

"This is for anyone who..."
- Is ready to start [achieving this outcome]
- Is tired of [common frustration]
- Is [doing this] but not seeing [desirable result]

"Now imagine if you could..."
- Stop struggling to...
- Finally feel confident...
- Get clarity on...

### What's Included
Summary of each module or list of things included.

Fields: Heading, Description, Call to Action

### Trust the Process
Step-by-step guide, keep it simple.

Fields: Step 1, Step 2, Step 3, Call to Action
