# Web Design OS

An AI-powered operating system for designing, building, and delivering websites. Use it to build your own site or run a full web design business - from lead to launch.

---

## What this is

Web Design OS is a vault that runs inside Claude Code. You open it, talk to Claude, and Claude runs the workflows that take you from a blank page to a finished project. It replaces:

- Notion templates full of empty fields
- ChatGPT prompts you copy and paste into browser tabs
- Separate tools for CRM, proposals, contracts, emails
- Hours of manual strategy and copywriting work

Everything lives in one place. Claude reads the frameworks, pulls the templates, and generates the deliverables in your voice using your client's data.

---

## Who it's for

Two types of user:

**Solopreneurs and creators** who want to build their own website or sales page without hiring a designer. You'll go through the same brand strategy and copywriting process that high-end agencies use, but guided by AI.

**Web design freelancers and agencies** who want to deliver professional websites in a fraction of the time. Full client management, AI client interviews, auto-generated strategy decks, email generation, and launch automation.

## How to use it

1. Clone this repo to your computer
2. Open the folder in Claude Code
3. Claude will automatically start the onboarding workflow
4. Follow the prompts - each step is a conversation

Once onboarding is done, just tell Claude what you want to do:

- "Run the AI interview for a new client"
- "Generate a homepage copy"
- "Create a proposal for Acme Corp"
- "Draft an email telling the client their designs are ready"

Claude knows the workflows and runs them for you.

---

## Updating

When new features ship, run:

```
update system
```

Claude pulls the latest workflows and tells you what's new. Your personal files are never touched.

---

## Two service delivery paths (client mode)

During onboarding you'll pick which paths you offer:

**Productized** - Fixed-price packages. Client buys, goes straight to the AI interview, you review and deliver. No discovery call, no proposal.

**Custom** - Bespoke high-ticket work. Discovery call, proposal, sign, AI interview, strategy refinement meeting (where you add the human insight that AI misses), then full strategy generation.

Most designers offer both.
