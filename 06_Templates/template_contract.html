Revisions
What's included

Included revisions. Two rounds of revisions are included at no extra charge, provided the changes do not substantially alter the original project scope, nature, or purpose, and provided final approval has not already been given. First round revisions are compiled on the day of the review meeting and delivered within 3 working days. Second round revisions are requested by the Client and delivered within 3 working days of the first round being completed.

Substantive changes. If the Client requests changes that would reasonably require more than 8 additional hours of work (as assessed in good faith by the Service Provider), the Service Provider is entitled to issue a separate invoice and scope amendment. Work on the revised services does not begin until the new invoice and scope amendment are signed and paid.

Cancellation
If either party ends the project

Either party may terminate this agreement by giving 7 days' written notice if the working relationship is deemed unsatisfactory.

In the event of cancellation by either party, the Service Provider retains ownership of all copyrights and original artwork, and the non-refundable Deposit is retained by the Service Provider. The Client is responsible for paying for the percentage of work completed as of the date of cancellation, as reasonably assessed by the Service Provider using the project timeline, milestones, and deliverables schedule as the basis for calculation. Any third-party expenses already incurred (stock photography, fonts, plugins, hosting, etc.) are also payable by the Client.
