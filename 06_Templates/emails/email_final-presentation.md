# Final Presentation

**When to send:** Delivering the final website presentation via Loom or other format.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Your website is ready to see
- Final website walkthrough inside
- [Business name] website - final review

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I know you've probably been waiting for this - your website is ready.

CLICK HERE to watch the presentation.

Of course, I want to make sure that you are 100% happy before you hit launch. So please take your time to also leave any FEEDBACK HERE.

I will then tweak any details if necessary. As always, if you have any questions just let me know.

We're almost there - can't wait to see you launch soon.

- Express genuine excitement about their project being nearly done
- Make the feedback link and presentation link crystal clear
