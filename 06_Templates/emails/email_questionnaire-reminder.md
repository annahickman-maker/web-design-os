# Questionnaire / Interview Reminder

**When to send:** Reminder to complete the AI interview or questionnaire before strategy meeting.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Gentle reminder for your brand questionnaire
- Quick check-in on your interview
- Looking forward to your strategy session

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I'm so excited to get started on your website. How are you getting on with filling in your brand questionnaire (or completing your interview)?

Just a reminder that it is due on or before [date] so we can get our project started on time.

As always, if you need help with this or have any questions just let me know.

I can't wait to go over your strategy with you on [date of strategy call].

See you then.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Keep the tone excited, not nagging
- Reference the upcoming meeting to create anticipation
