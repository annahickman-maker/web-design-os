# Support Check-in

**When to send:** 5 days before post-launch support period ends.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Your support period ends soon
- Final check-in for [business name]
- Questions before support ends?

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Just a friendly reminder that your free 30-day support is ending soon.

If you have any last minute questions about your website, please send them over so I can help you before [date].

Thank you.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Keep it short and friendly
- Offer help one last time
- Mention the specific end date
