# Testimonial Request

**When to send:** 4-6 weeks after launch - enough time for results.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Quick favour - how's the website doing?
- Checking in on your new website
- Could I ask for your help?

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I hope everything is good with you.

It's been a while now since we finished your project - how are things going with your business?

I hope that your website has helped you with [goal] so far.

I was wondering if you'd be willing to take a minute to answer a few questions about your experience of working together:

1. What were you struggling with in your business prior to our project?

2. Have your business revenue/bookings/enquiries/prices increased since working with us?

3. How did your website help you achieve your business goals?

4. How have your feelings about your business changed since working with us?

5. If you were recommending us to a friend, what would you say about your experience working with us?

Thank you so much - this really helps a lot.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Reference their specific goals from the original project
- Ask how they are first - shows care
- Specific questions get specific answers (much better than "leave a testimonial")
- Keep the ask light
