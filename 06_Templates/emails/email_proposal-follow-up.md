# Proposal Follow-up

**When to send:** 48 hours after sending the proposal if no response.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Quick follow-up on your proposal
- Just checking in on [project name]
- Any questions about the proposal?

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I just wanted to check that you were able to access your proposal. I am keen to see what you think and answer any questions for you.

Again - just in case you missed it, you can CLICK HERE to access your client portal.

Once you're on there I've recorded a short video guiding you through the proposal and next steps.

Please note that this proposal is only valid until [deadline].

Looking forward to hearing back from you and getting started.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Keep it light - don't make them feel guilty
- Mention the validity deadline to create gentle urgency
- Offer to answer questions
