# Not a Good Fit

**When to send:** Politely decline a lead - either from their initial inquiry or after a discovery call.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Thanks for reaching out
- About your [project type] project

## Variant 1: Based on Inquiry Form

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Thanks so much for taking the time to fill out the contact form on my website. I'm so happy that you want to work together.

Based on your [budget/timeline/requirements], I think that your project may be better suited to another website designer.

[Optional: If you like, I can refer you to [designer], who might be a better fit for this project.]

Again, thank you so much for expressing an interest in working together. I wish you all the best in your business.

## Variant 2: Based on Sales Call

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

It was really great meeting you today, thank you for taking the time to get on a call.

But after considering your [budget/timeline/requirements], I think that your project may be better suited to another website designer.

[Optional: If you like, I can refer you to [designer], who might be a better fit for this project.]

Again, thank you so much for expressing an interest in working together. I wish you all the best in your business.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Be warm but direct
- Don't apologise excessively - this protects both parties
- Only offer a referral if you genuinely know someone who'd be a better fit
- Keep the door open for the future if appropriate
