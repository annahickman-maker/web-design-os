# Website Content

**When to send:** After strategy meeting - deliver the draft copy for client review, or send copy templates.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Your website content is ready
- Review your copy for [business name]
- Next steps - website content

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Hope you're having a great week so far.

I know you've probably been waiting for this - so here is your website content to review.

Based on what we discussed in our strategy meeting, I have taken care to include as much detail as possible.

However, there were a few gaps in the content that I will need your help to fill.

Here's what I'll need you to do:

1. Review your website content HERE, leave your feedback next to each section and try your best to fill in any gaps in the content.
2. Upload your images and brand assets HERE.

I will then tweak any details if necessary.

Please submit both of these on or before [date] so we can stay on track to launch your website on time.

As always, keep me posted on your progress and don't hesitate to reach out to me if you have any questions.

Thank you.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Reference specific messaging or directions discussed in the strategy meeting
- Be clear about what you need from them
- Set a deadline but keep it reasonable
