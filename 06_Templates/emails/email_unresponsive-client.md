# Unresponsive Client

**When to send:** Client has missed deadlines or gone silent.

## Variant 1: Project on Hold (3+ days after missed deadline)

**Subject suggestions:**
- Update on your project
- Project status - [business name]
- Quick update on next steps

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I hope everything is ok with you.

Unfortunately, I have to inform you that I'm placing our website design project on hold until your:

[If invoice overdue:]
outstanding invoice is paid. [Invoice number] of [amount] has been past due for [days] now.

[If feedback/content overdue:]
[deliverable] feedback is returned.

I understand that delays can happen, but as I haven't heard from you then I am unable to continue with the project. We can resume work again as soon as your [invoice is paid/feedback is ready].

If you have any questions or concerns please let me know.

Looking forward to hearing from you.

## Variant 2: Project Reschedule (still silent after on-hold email)

**Subject suggestions:**
- Your project spot
- Final update on [business name] project

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I hope everything is well with you.

I haven't heard from you in quite some time now, and so unfortunately at this point I can no longer hold your project spot without being unfair to my other clients.

Of course, if you would like to resume your project, I'm still open to rescheduling in the future.

We can continue where we left off for an additional fee, as stated in the terms of our contract. Let me know if you'd like to schedule a call so we can discuss where to go from here.

Regardless of what happens next, I wish you all the best for your business.

## Variant 3: Project Terminated (difficult client)

**Subject suggestions:**
- Ending our project
- Final notice - project cancellation

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I'm writing to inform you that I'd like to terminate our project, effective immediately.

I have done my best to reach a solution for your website that you are happy with, but despite our efforts we've been unable to come to an agreement on the project's direction and timeline.

It may be time for you to find another designer that would be better suited to meet your expectations.

I appreciate all the time you have put into collaborating on this with me and am grateful for the opportunity to work together.

I wish you all the best for your business.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Stay firm but professional
- Reference contract terms where appropriate
- Keep the tone respectful even when ending difficult relationships
- Document everything in the client file
