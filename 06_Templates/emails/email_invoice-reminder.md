# Invoice Reminder

**When to send:** Payment reminder before or after due date.

## Variant 1: First Reminder

**Subject suggestions:**
- Payment reminder for [business name]
- Your invoice due [date]
- Quick payment reminder

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Just a friendly reminder that your [deposit/final payment] is due on [date].

In case you missed it, you can view your invoice and make your payment HERE.

Please let me know if you have any questions or require any additional information.

## Variant 2: Overdue Reminder

**Subject suggestions:**
- Outstanding invoice for your project
- Payment needed to continue
- Invoice reminder - payment overdue

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

How are things with you?

Just a reminder that your latest invoice due on [date] is still outstanding.

You can view your invoice and make your payment HERE.

Please let me know if you have any questions or concerns. Otherwise, until this is paid we will be unable to move forward with the project as planned.

Thank you.

**Personalisation notes:**
- First reminder should be friendly and light
- Overdue reminder should be firm but not aggressive
- Always offer to help if there's an issue
