# Delivering Designs

**When to send:** Present designs to client and ask for feedback.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Your designs are ready
- [Project name] - homepage designs
- Take a look at your new website

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Hope you're having a great week so far.

I know you've probably been waiting for this - so here is your [deliverable].

Based on what we discussed in our strategy meeting, I have taken care to include as much detail as possible.

Please take your time to look over and leave your FEEDBACK HERE.

I will then tweak any details if necessary, to make sure you are 100% happy with the direction before moving onto [next stage].

Thank you.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Match the deliverable type: homepage, full website, brand direction, etc.
- Include the specific feedback link or instructions
- Set expectations for the next stage
