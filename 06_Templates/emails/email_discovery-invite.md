# Discovery Call Invite

**When to send:** New lead is a good fit - respond to their inquiry.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Quick chat about your [project type]?
- Excited to chat about your website
- Let's talk about [client business]

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Thanks so much for taking the time to fill out the contact form on my website, I'm so happy that you want to work together.

I would love to set up a call with you in the next couple of weeks to hear more about your goals for [INSERT PROJECT DETAILS].

It should only take around 30 minutes to chat about your business and explore how we can work together.

If you'd like to come prepared, you can bring with you some website inspirations for aesthetics and functionality - this would help a lot.

Below is the link to my calendar so you can select a time that works for you:

[INSERT CALENDLY LINK]

Looking forward to chatting soon.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Reference something specific they mentioned in their inquiry
- Adjust greeting warmth to match designer voice
- If you know why they're reaching out now, acknowledge it
