# Referral Request

**When to send:** After client has had time to see results and relationship is warm.

## Variant 1: Past Client Referral

**Subject suggestions:**
- Saw your [topic] post
- Amazing work with [topic]
- Quick question

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I just saw your post about [topic] on [social media] - and I wanted to check in and see how everything is going?

It looks like things are going great for you, I'm so happy that I got to play a (small) part in your journey.

I was also thinking how much I would love to work on more website projects like yours. If you know of anyone like you who would benefit from a web designer - would you be willing to refer them?

If so, I have a referral offer of [detail offer] that you could both benefit from. If anyone comes to mind, here is a referral template you can use:

"Hey [name] - I wanted to connect you with [name]. They worked on our website last year and since then we have seen [growth/results]. Highly recommend if you are looking for an expert to help you grow your online presence."

Thank you so much. And if there's anything else I can do for you, please don't hesitate to ask.

## Variant 2: Another Service Provider Referral Partnership

**Subject suggestions:**
- Love your work - potential partnership?
- Referral partnership idea
- Big fan of your content

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I've been following your content for a while now, and I just saw your post on [topic]. I love this [elaborate on why it resonated with you].

As a web designer for [target audience], my clients often ask me if I can recommend a [service provider]. I was wondering if your clients ever ask you if you have a referral for a web designer?

If so, maybe you would be interested in a referral partnership?

Either way, I'd love to chat and exchange ideas from one service provider to another. If this is something you'd be interested in, here's a link to my calendar so you can find a time that works for you.

Looking forward to (hopefully) talking soon.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Past client: reference something specific they recently shared
- Service provider: show you actually follow their work
- Make the ask clear but not demanding
- Always offer value in return
