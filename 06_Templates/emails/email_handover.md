# Handover

**When to send:** After final payment - congratulate and deliver all assets.

**Subject suggestions:**
- Your website is live
- Handover and training for [business name]
- Congratulations on your new website

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Thank you so much for making your final payment - I have just turned over all the assets to you, including the ownership of the website itself.

You can find all the final files and website tutorials in your client portal HERE. These will help you to customise and maintain your website on your own.

But if you ever need my help outside of these videos, I am available for 30-day support as promised.

Can't wait for your big launch - don't forget to share it with us on [social account] so we can help promote the big day.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Express genuine excitement for their launch
- Remind them about the support period
- Provide specific links to training materials
- Encourage them to tag you when they launch
