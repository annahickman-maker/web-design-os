# Feedback Follow-up

**When to send:** Following up based on the type of feedback received (or lack of feedback).

## Variant 1: No Feedback (3+ days after sending)

**Subject suggestions:**
- Any feedback on your designs?
- Checking in on [deliverable]

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I just wanted to check that you were able to access your [deliverable]. I am keen to see what you think and answer any questions for you.

Again - just in case you missed it, you can CLICK HERE to access [strategy/web design presentation].

Once you're in here, there is also a space to leave any feedback.

Please submit your feedback on or before [date] so we can stay on track to launch your website on time.

As always, if you have any questions or concerns just let me know.

## Variant 2: Good Feedback

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to leave your feedback on [deliverable]. I am SO excited that you love our direction so far.

If there's nothing else you'd like to add or change, I will get to work on [next deliverable] and you will have this by [date].

So keep an eye on your inbox - and have a great week.

## Variant 3: Bad Feedback

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to leave your feedback on [deliverable].

I hear your concerns, and want to assure you that I will be doing everything I can to get the [deliverable] to a place where you're happy with it before we move forward.

I will be working to implement the changes you requested by [date].

In the meantime, if you have any more questions or concerns please let me know.

## Variant 4: Vague Feedback

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to leave your feedback on [deliverable].

I want to ensure that I'm doing everything I can to get the [deliverable] to a place where you're happy with it before we move forward, so there are a few points I'd like to clarify.

Could you please elaborate on the following?

[List points]

As soon as I hear back from you, I will get to work implementing these changes.

Thank you.

**Personalisation notes:**
- Match the variant to the actual feedback received
- Stay warm and professional even in the bad feedback scenario
- For vague feedback, ask specific questions rather than "can you clarify"
